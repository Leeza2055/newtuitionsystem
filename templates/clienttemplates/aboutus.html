about us

Our Objective

To provide a better platform for students to help them in their study and find a suitable teacher according to their needs who will ensure their growth and welfare.

To provide job opportunities for teachers who are willing to teach and help the students grow in a friendly environment.

How This Works

A tutor creates his profile in the site by going to registration page. The information like his academic qualifications, teaching experiences etc are provided.

Tutors information can be visible to parents/students through our site.


Parents/students can search for the tutors relevant to them in their locations.
